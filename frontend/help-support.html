Live Monitoring Issues

Ensure your internet connection is stable. If feeds don't load, check if the camera is online in your dashboard.

Predictions Accuracy

AI predictions get better over time. Allow up to 48 hours for the model to calibrate to a newly added location.

Notification Settings

You can customize your alerts in the Settings tab to avoid being notified for low-severity density warnings.

Account Access

If you lose access to your account, you can use the Reset Password option on the login screen to recover it.
